Alexander and Joseph: Adrift



Scenario

After a long journey in NASA's newest experimental space shuttle, you've hit some space debris. You're now stranded on a planet that conveniently enough has the perfect environment for you to survive. Repair your spaceship and return to Earth.



Controls

WASD keys to move forwards, left, right, and backwards respectively. Alternatively, if you so desired, you can use the arrow keys to move their respective directions. Left mouse button to click and interact with collectible items.



Gameplay



Objective

Collect the three items required to repair your spaceship: a gem for fuel, the tablet for ship controls, and your ai autopilot to navigate space.
